German Pancakes

An easy anytime meal!

When I was a young lad my father took me to the city to see the marching band. He said "Someday you grow up and learn that youth isn't forever, one day I'll die, and the same with you; wouldn't you want to fill the time you have with delicious german pancakes?"

Ingredients

6 eggs An ungodly amount of butter
Milk Flour
(optional) Cinnamon (optional) Strawberries

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Melt the butter into the pan(microwave or over the stove works)
  3. Mix eggs, milk, and flour(adding the flour slowly)
  4. Pour mixture into pan and put pan in oven
  5. pull out after 30 mins and serve with syrup
